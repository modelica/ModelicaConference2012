The Modelica Conference is the main event for users, library developers, tool vendors and language designers to share their knowledge and learn about the latest scientific and industrial progress related to Modelica and to the Functional Mockup Interface.

This program covers modeling of complex physical and cyber-physical systems, as well as tools, for a wide range of research and industrial applications. The conference proceedings contain all accepted conference abstracts as well as the submitted full proceedings papers corresponding to regular presentations or poster presentations.
